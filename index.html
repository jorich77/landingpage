My Story

From Burnout to Freedom

Years in the 9–5 grind left me feeling drained, uninspired, and constantly on the edge of burnout. I was tired of trading time for money, missing out on quality moments with my family, and juggling the never-ending demands of motherhood and full-time working.

"It felt like I was sleepwalking through life — functioning, but not truly living."

As a couple, we wanted time to do what we love, be present with our kids, and prioritize our family's wellbeing over busyness. Can you relate?

That desire sparked a big change. We started an online wellbeing business that supports our family's health and gives us the freedom to travel full-time.

Now, I help other moms create real time and financial freedom — without sacrificing what matters most.
