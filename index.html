Our Story

Hi, we're Stew & Jo

We're a family who decided that life was too short to live on repeat. So we did something a little wild — we sold everything, left the UK, and swapped the everyday grind for full-time travel, adventure, and freedom.

"This wasn't just about seeing the world. It was about reclaiming our life — our time, our energy, and the moments with our kids we knew we could never get back."

Jo's Story

Jo spent years in the 9–5 grind which left her feeling drained, uninspired, and constantly on the edge of burnout. Tired of trading time for money, missing out on quality moments with family, and juggling the never-ending demands of motherhood and full-time work.

Stew's Story

Stew overcame a cancer diagnosis in 2018, and since then has been committed to rebuilding his health from the inside out. This journey led him to a more natural, intentional approach to both physical and mental wellbeing.

Why Enagic?

After discovering Kangen Water as part of Stew's recovery, we fell in love with the products and the community. We only promote things we genuinely use and believe in — and Enagic ticked every box.
