📋 Calculator Assumptions
Rank-Based Commission:
Commission per sale = Your Rank × Base Commission Rate. For example, a 3A distributor earns 3 points per sale (3 × $351 = $1,053 for K8 in USA). This applies equally to both direct and indirect sales.
Rank Advancement:
Ranks are based on total sales count (direct + indirect combined). All products count as 1 sale each.

1A = 1-2 sales, 2A = 3-10 sales, 3A = 11-20 sales, 4A = 21-50 sales, 5A = 51-100 sales, 6A = 101+ sales.
Commission Rates:
All commission rates include Special Points (SP) and assume regular direct sales activity. Rates shown are "Basic + SP" combined values. Important: Figures do NOT include the 2% Enagic Business Support Fee (effective May 1, 2026) which will be deducted from actual commissions paid.
Projection Model:
This calculator projects monthly income based on consistent sales volumes. It does not account for rank advancement timing during the month, commission payment schedules, or variable growth patterns.

This is an estimating tool only and should not be used as an accurate calculator. Commission and income calculations are estimates based on the information provided and may not reflect actual earnings.
